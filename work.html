04. Proof

Case studies.

Every one of these started with the same problem: good work that wasn't being remembered.

ACAD AI: School Outreach

Reaching 50+ institutions cold, and turning several into demos.

When you're selling to someone who's never heard of you, positioning determines whether the conversation even begins.

Cold calling school administrators about a product they've never heard of is a positioning problem before it's a sales problem. I built the scripts, objection handling, and follow-up sequences, and used them to reach over 50 institutions directly.

10+
Demos booked
from cold outreach
Freelance Copywriting: Pitch Deck & Brand Copy

Turning vague copy into a meeting.

People don't act on vague enthusiasm. They act on a clear ask.

A client came to me with copy doing the opposite of its job: vague language, no clear offer, no clear benefit. I rebuilt the language around a clear offer and a clear ask instead of general statements about what they did.

1
Meeting landed, plus
a client referral
ACAD AI: Re-engagement Messaging

Bringing a third of ghosted leads back.

Silence isn't a no. It's usually just an unfinished conversation.

Leads that go quiet aren't usually gone. They're often just unconvinced the follow-up is worth responding to. I built WhatsApp re-engagement messaging specifically for leads who'd already gone cold.

~33%
Of cold leads
responded & re-engaged
ACAD AI: Educators', Students' & OUR UYO Communities

Building communities people actually return to.

People don't stay in communities for content. They stay for consistency.

I managed three communities at once, each needing a different voice for a different audience, but the same discipline: consistent value, not just noise. For the educators' community, I built a repeatable system: monthly calendars, a 70/30 value-to-engagement framework, verified resources grounded in real Nigerian education context.

3
Communities run
at once, still active today

Cold Call Script

"Good evening ma. My name is Treasure. Please, am I speaking with the Principal of [School Name]?"

With schools already using CBT, the same three problems kept coming up: platforms crashing when many students test at once, result processing that still takes time because theory scripts are marked by hand, and students who never see where they went wrong, question by question. This script opens with that, not with a pitch.
